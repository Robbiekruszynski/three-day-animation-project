Griffon Vulture

The griffon vulture is a large Old World vulture in the bird of prey family Accipitridae. It is also known as the Eurasian griffon. It is not to be confused with a different species, Rüppell's griffon vulture. It is closely related to the white-backed vulture. -Altitude - 37,000ft -Wingspan: 7.5 – 9.2 ft. -Family: Accipitridae -Conservation status: Least Concern (Population increasing) Encyclopedia of Life -Scientific name: Gyps fulvus -Mass: Female: 14 – 25 lbs, Male: 14 – 23 lbs
